Who We Are

Village Heating was founded in 1992 by David Kelly with one clear purpose: to raise the standard of care, compliance, and customer service in the heating and property maintenance industry. What began as a family-run business has grown into a trusted, multi-trade contractor supporting clients across both the private and social housing sectors

For over 30 years, we’ve built our reputation on trust, performance, and a resident-first mindset — delivering heating, plumbing, electrical, and compliance services to thousands of homes, housing estates, and commercial sites. Our team works closely with individual homeowners, managing agents, and housing providers to deliver reliable, regulation-ready work backed by qualified engineers and transparent reporting.

Today, we operate across London, the South East, and the wider Midlands corridor — combining technical expertise with digital innovation to deliver services that are safe, sustainable, and future-ready.

Our Timeline

Our work is guided by four non-negotiable values:

1992

Founded with Purpose

Village Heating is established by David Kelly as a family-run heating business, focused on domestic boiler installations and servicing in London.

2005

Expands Into Plumbing & Reactive Repairs

Growing demand leads to the launch of plumbing and reactive maintenance services, supporting local landlords and block managers

2012

Entry into Social Housing Contracts

Awarded first local authority contract for heating maintenance and compliance services — marking the beginning of our social housing division.

2017

Multi-Trade Capability Introduced

Electrical and water hygiene services added, positioning Village Heating as a one-stop compliance and maintenance provider for housing associations and ALMOs.

2020

Digital Transformation & SLA Dashboards

Launch of digital job tracking, real-time SLA reporting, and client dashboards to support frameworks and performance-based contracts.

2024

Framework-Approved & Net Zero Focused

Now a trusted framework contractor with full multi-region coverage. Delivering Net Zero-aligned solutions and supporting over 40,000 annual visits across private and public housing stock.
